Social Info

Where does our funding go?

All donors can personalize their contributions by selecting which categories they would like to donate to.

Academics

Funding for academics is extremely important to use as we put it towards learning materials, technology, and other resources to improve the learning experience of each child.

Athletics

Funding for athletics truly helps in promoting a healthy and unified school environment as it allows for new and additional equipment, athletic workshops and other activities!

Environment

Environment Funding for our school is used to maintain the homestead of Nile Academy. We want to ensure that Nile is well maintained both for the comfort, safety, and aesthetic enjoyment of our school community.
