SosudaNile Academy’s History

We offer a wide range of high quality of teaching and extra-curricular activities...

SosudaNile Academy was established in 2021 with the goal of providing better education and a brighter future for children. It prides itself in its experienced staff who are determined to establish a positive learning culture and a dynamic educational vision. SosudaNile Staff work diligently to enable exceptional results in student learning and take great careassessing student needs. The school has built its reputation on catering for individual student needs and as well as its diversity. We welcome families of all beliefs and cultural backgrounds.

Our school will prepare your child not only to achieve greatness but to live their life with a sense of moral and ethical responsibility. By providing a private school environment with small class sizes and low student to teacher ratio, while also following the Ontario Curriculum, we can ensure our students are well prepared for the rest of their educational pursuits here in Canada. Our school also welcomes and provides boarding for international students.

Mission And Vision

SosudaNile Academy’s mission is to enable its students to reach the highest levels of achievement. We aim to develop intellectual and personal qualities so our students can become confident, effective and responsible citizens of the world. Our vision is to offer our students the most outstanding education we possibly can.

Why SosudaNile Academy?

Nile Academy is committed to providing its students with a safe and secure learning environment where they will be challenged to achieve the highest academic standards set by the Ontario Ministry of Education’s school curriculum, with a strong emphasis on the Sciences and Information and Communication Technology.

  • A safe, secure, and nurturing environment conducive to learning.
  • Intellectually stimulating and challenging learning activities.
  • A high value placed on academic excellence and achievement.
  • An Ontario Ministry of Education school curriculum.
  • Supervised after-school programs, enrichment and leadership activities.
  • Students acquire a good self-esteem, self-confidence and the joy of learning
  • Well-qualified and enthusiastic teachers.

SosudaNile Academy seeks an active partnership between school and family based on a shared commitment to the students’ goals, values and the special joys of childhood. All members of the SosudaNile Academy community are asked to hold each other to high expectations of behavior and achievement so that our children will aspire to make a better world. Join us and be a part of a dynamic learning environment. We strive for excellence!
